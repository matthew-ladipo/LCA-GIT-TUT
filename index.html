Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. 1.
    Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. 2.
    Heat the pan: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. 3.
    Cook the eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and let them cook undisturbed for a few seconds. Then, gently stir with a spatula until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny.
  4. 4.
    Add fillings: If using, add your choice of fillings to one half of the omelette. Fold the other half over the fillings and cook for another minute until the eggs are fully set.
  5. 5.
    Serve: Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.
